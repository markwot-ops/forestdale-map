## Standing Rules
- Forestdale uses `"story"` field
- No birth dates in narratives
- No "is buried at" line
- Standard close: "If you have further information about this veteran, please contact the City of Holyoke Veterans Graves Officer at (413) 322-5630 at 310 Appleton Street, 1st Floor, Holyoke, MA 01040."
- Photos at repo root, no subfolders
- All "2" photos are secondary/family stones — use extraPhotos field
